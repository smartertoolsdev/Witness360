How it works

Record and stream at the same time in different resolutions, even with the screen locked/closed or the app in the background.
If your phone is lost, stolen or damaged, your streamed footage stays safely stored on the RTMP (stream) server. Share your live stream so others can follow in real time.
For safety/emergency use, enable the "Record & Stream on Start" option, to record/stream automatically on app launch.

What can this app do?

  • Record & stream at the same time with different resolutions and custom FPS and bitrate
  • Record & stream with closed/locked screen and app in the background
  • Option to auto record & stream on app start
  • Switch between front & back camera while recording/streaming
  • Dark mode with low FPS
  • Use flashlight while recording/streaming
  • Automatically retry if a stream connection fails
  • Protect videos from low-battery shutdowns and accidental swipes
  • Store videos in internal storage with optional code lock
  • Open custom number to place a call
  • Portrait & landscape orientations
  • Zoom support
  • Customizable interface
  • ..and many more on the way!
