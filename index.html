How it works

Record and stream at the same time in different resolutions, even with the screen locked/closed or the app minimized.
If your phone is lost, stolen or damaged, your stream footage remains safely stored on the RTMP server. Share your live stream with viewers to follow in real time.
For safety or emergency use, enable the auto record & stream on app start.

What can this app do?

  • Record & stream to RTMP endpoint at the same time with different resolutions and custom FPS and bitrate
  • Record & stream with closed/locked screen and minimized app
  • Option to auto record & stream on app start
  • Switch camera while recording/streaming
  • Darkness mode with low FPS
  • Use flashlight while recording/streaming
  • Automatically retry if a stream connection fails
  • Protect videos from low-battery shutdowns and accidental swipes
  • Store videos in internal storage with optional code lock
  • Open custom number to place a call
  • Portrait & landscape orientations
  • Zoom support
  • Customizable interface
